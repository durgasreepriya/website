Welcome to Tamil Nadu Integrated Welfare Portal

This portal serves as a one-stop digital gateway to access all government welfare schemes in Tamil Nadu. Our mission is to ensure that every eligible citizen can easily access, apply for, and benefit from the various welfare programs being implemented by the Tamil Nadu government.

The welfare schemes featured on this portal are designed to uplift vulnerable communities, support economic development, and promote social equity across all districts of Tamil Nadu. Through this integrated platform, you can learn about eligibility criteria, and connect directly with government officials for assistance.

Social Welfare Schemes

Supporting vulnerable sections of society through targeted assistance programs including pension schemes, educational support, and healthcare benefits. These initiatives ensure social security for the elderly, disabled, widows, and other vulnerable groups.

Visit Official Portal

Marriage Assistance Scheme

Financial assistance and gold for marriage of young women from economically weaker sections, including special provisions for graduates. This scheme has helped thousands of families cover marriage expenses while promoting higher education among girls.

Visit Official Portal

Farmers Welfare Scheme

Comprehensive support for farmers including crop insurance, equipment subsidies, and drought relief assistance. These programs are vital for agricultural sustainability and food security in Tamil Nadu, benefiting over 1.5 million farming families.

Visit Official Portal

Youth Entrepreneurship Program

Supporting young entrepreneurs through financial assistance, training, and mentorship for starting small businesses. This flagship program has successfully created over 20,000 new businesses since its inception and continues to drive economic growth across the state.

How to Apply

For all schemes, applications can be submitted through the respective department's official portal. You can also visit your nearest e-Seva center or Common Service Center for assistance with the application process.

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: How do I check my application status?

A: Visit the official portal of the respective scheme and use your application ID to track the status.

Q: What documents are typically required?

A: Common requirements include Aadhaar card, income certificate, residence proof, and scheme-specific documents.

Q: How long does the application process take?

A: Processing times vary by scheme, but most applications are processed within 30-45 days.
